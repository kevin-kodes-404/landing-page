Welcome to Kevin's Coffee Corner
Here you will find the best we have to offer. From single origin beans, to artful cappuccinos, and delicious baked goods. This is your one-stop shop for all your caffeine needs.
Why you're here.
Our famous cappucinos are made by experienced baristas. The playful art is just a bonus. Alternative milk choices available.
Coffee beans are important. That's why we only source coffee beans from the best places on earth. Come try our exclusive single-origin Guatemalan coffee today!
What's coffee's best friend? A warm, freshly-baked pastry from our own bakery. Try our croissants, cinnamon buns, and seasonal muffins!
Enjoy the company of familiar faces. Or new ones (we see you first-date people). Feel at home in our comfy couches and chairs while you sip the day away.
"Coffee isn't just a warm drink. It's an invitation to slow down, be patient, and be present. Here at Kevin's Coffee Corner, we provide the best coffee, the cozy furniture and the ambience. You bring community, conversations, and laughter that fills our walls. We can't wait to welcome you!"
-Kevin, Owner and Coffee Lover
